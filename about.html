About IronForge Industries

Leading the steel industry with innovation and excellence

Our Story

Founded in 1974, IronForge Industries began as a small family-owned steel fabrication shop with a simple mission: to deliver the highest quality steel products with unmatched customer service. Over the decades, we have grown into one of the region's most trusted steel manufacturers while maintaining our commitment to excellence and personal touch.

What started as a two-person operation has evolved into a state-of-the-art facility employing over 100 skilled professionals. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, strategic investments in cutting-edge technology, and an unwavering dedication to sustainable manufacturing practices.

Today, IronForge Industries stands as a testament to the power of vision, hard work, and commitment to quality. We serve clients across multiple industries, from construction and infrastructure to automotive and aerospace, always delivering solutions that exceed expectations.

Our Mission

To forge excellence in steel manufacturing by delivering innovative, sustainable, and high-quality products that empower our clients to build a better tomorrow while maintaining the highest standards of safety and environmental responsibility.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in steel innovation, recognized for our commitment to sustainability, technological advancement, and exceptional customer partnerships that shape the future of construction and manufacturing.

Our Values

  • Quality without compromise
  • Innovation through collaboration
  • Sustainability in every process
  • Integrity in all relationships
  • Safety as our foundation
