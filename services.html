Our Services

Comprehensive steel solutions for every industry need

Complete Steel Manufacturing Solutions

At IronForge Industries, we offer a comprehensive range of steel manufacturing and processing services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients across various industries. Our state-of-the-art facilities and expert team ensure precision, quality, and reliability in every project.

Custom Steel Fabrication

Our custom fabrication services combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to create precise steel components tailored to your specifications. From structural elements to intricate architectural features, we bring your vision to life.

  • Structural steel fabrication
  • Architectural metalwork
  • Custom brackets and supports
  • Welding and assembly
  • Precision cutting and shaping

CNC Machining Services

Our advanced CNC machining capabilities ensure exceptional precision and consistency for complex steel components. With state-of-the-art equipment and skilled operators, we deliver parts that meet the tightest tolerances.

  • Multi-axis CNC machining
  • Precision turning and milling
  • Prototype development
  • Large-scale production runs
  • Quality inspection and testing

Heat Treatment Services

Our comprehensive heat treatment services enhance the mechanical properties of steel products, improving hardness, strength, and durability to meet specific application requirements.

  • Annealing and normalizing
  • Hardening and tempering
  • Stress relieving
  • Carburizing and nitriding
  • Controlled atmosphere processing

Surface Coating & Protection

Protect your steel investments with our comprehensive coating solutions. From galvanizing to specialized industrial coatings, we provide long-lasting protection against corrosion and environmental damage.

  • Hot-dip galvanizing
  • Powder coating
  • Industrial painting
  • Zinc plating
  • Specialized protective coatings

Quality Testing & Inspection

Our comprehensive quality assurance program ensures that every product meets or exceeds industry standards. We employ advanced testing methods and maintain strict quality control protocols.

  • Non-destructive testing
  • Chemical composition analysis
  • Mechanical property testing
  • Dimensional inspection
  • Certification and documentation

Logistics & Delivery

Our efficient logistics network ensures timely delivery of your steel products. With real-time tracking and flexible scheduling options, we make sure your materials arrive when and where you need them.

  • Just-in-time delivery
  • Global shipping network
  • Real-time tracking
  • Secure packaging
  • Expedited service options
